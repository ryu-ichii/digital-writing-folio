# digital-writing-folio
## Biographical Note
I am a writer of mostly fiction and creative nonfiction. My current selection of writing tools is limited: MS Word, pen and paper, the notes app and personal experience. I write for some sort of self-preservation and understanding, thus my creativity is limited to attempting to make sense of my own experience. MS Word and notepads serve their purpose as containers for the lines of words — a page I can see my writing without subversion or interruption. Although this process neglects a whole realm of possibility.

I am content with my current selection of tools but am strangled both literally and imaginatively by their limitations. I write for the intention of print and do not consider the words as something that could engage with computerisation or require active interaction from their reader. I believe this is an out-of-date and apprehensive approach that I would like to pull free from. Here, I hope to develop prose and poetry in conversation and conjunction with the new and expansive digital world.
