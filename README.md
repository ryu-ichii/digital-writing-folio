# digital-writing-folio
## Biographical Note
I am a writer of fiction and creative nonfiction, although I do occasionally wrestle with poetry. My current selection of writing tools is limited: MS Word, the notes app and personal experience. These are the only tools I currently wield throughout the writing process. I write to find truth and my creativity is limited to attempting to make sense of my own experience. MS Word and notes serve their purpose as containers for the lines of words — a page I can see my writing without subversion or interruption. I am content with my current selection of tools but am contrained both physically and imaginatively by their limitations. I write for the intention of print publication and do not consider the words as something that require computation or active interaction. I think this is an out-of-date and possibly apprehensive approach that I would like to pull free from. I hope to develop more fiction, nonfiction and poetry in a new and expansive digital world.
